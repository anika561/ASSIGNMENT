Baked Vegetables Recipe:

The Ingredients Lists

  • MAGGI Magic Cubes (Vegetarian)
    2, crumbled

  • Milk
    3 cup (450 ml)

  • Maida
    3 tablespoons

  • Butter
    2 tablespoons

  • Chopped Vegetables (Cauliflower, Beans, Peas, Carrot, Corn)
    3 cups

  • Cheese
    1 cup,grated

  • Salt
    To taste

  • Pepper
    To taste

  • Toasted Bread Crumbs
    2 tablespoons

Easy steps to make:

  1. In a pan,pour milk and add maida. Mix well to breal any lumps and bring it to a boil,
    stirring continously.Add the grated cheese and blend well.Keep aside.

  2. In another pan,melt butter,add the vegetables and saute for 3-4 mins. Add the sauteed
    vegetables to the cheese sause, mix well. Add crumbled MAGGI Magic Cubes(Vegetarian).
    Adjust salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Pour into a glass dish.Top with toasted bread crumbs and bake in a hot oven for 5 mins.
    Serve hot.

Healthy Soya Salad Recipe:

The Ingredients List

  • MAGGI Masala-ae-Magic
    1 sachet

  • Chickpeas(Kabuli Chana)
    ½ cup

  • Soya Granules
    ½ cup

  • Onion
    1 small, chopped

  • Tomato
    1 small, chopped

  • Chaat Masala
    ¼ teaspoon

  • Salt
    To Taste

  • Oil
    1 tablespoon

  • Lime Juice
    1 tablespoon

  • Coriander Leaves
    2 tablespoons, finely chopped

Easy steps to make:

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight and pressure cook along with raw peanuts for 10.
    Soak soya granules in warm water for 10 mins, press them gently and take out excess water.

  2. In bowl take chickpea, peanuts, soya granules, onion, tomato, MAGGI Masala-ae-Magic,
    chaat masala, salt, oil and mix well.

  3. Garnish with coriander leaves and lemon juice, serve chilled.
