RAJASTHAN

1. Rajasthan is often known as the most colour-coordinated state. The popular cities of Rajasthan are devoted to a separate colour; Jaipur is Pink, Jodhpur is Blue, Udaipur is White and Jhalawar is Purple.
2. Rajasthan is the largest state of India by area with the total area of 34,239 sq km.
3. The best way to enjoy the desert safari in Rajasthan is the traditional way, which is through a camel ride.

2. Medieval Punjab saw a supremacy of the Muslims.
